About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization established in 2020. We are a youth-driven NGO working across India to solve real social problems through structured programs.

Our team includes volunteers, interns, and field workers who actively participate in community development projects focused on sustainable impact.

Our Mission

🎯 Empower Youth

Provide internships, skill development, and leadership opportunities.

🤝 Social Equality

Support underprivileged communities through direct intervention programs.
