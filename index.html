About Us

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact.

We work across India through a network of volunteers and professionals dedicated to education, animal welfare, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, and youth development.
