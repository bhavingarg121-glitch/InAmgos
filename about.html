InAmigos Foundation

Creating Lasting Social Impact Across India

About Us

InAmigos Foundation, founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating meaningful and sustainable social impact across India. Based in Chhattisgarh, we operate nationwide through a strong network of volunteers and professionals.

Our Credentials

  • Section 8 Registered Organization (Government of India Licensed)
  • 80G & 12A Certified – Tax benefits for donors
  • CSR-1 Registered – Eligible for corporate CSR partnerships
  • NITI Aayog Registered – Aligned with national development goals
  • IAF ISO 9001:2015 Certified – Quality management standards

Our Key Initiatives

  • Project SEVA – Food and clothing distribution for the underprivileged.
  • Project BACHPANSHALA – Education, digital literacy & life skills for children.
  • Project JEEV – Animal welfare and feeding programs for stray animals.
  • Project UDAAN – Women empowerment through skills & self-help groups.
  • Project PRAKRITI – Environmental conservation & tree plantation drives.
  • Project VIKAS – Skill development & internship programs for youth.

Our Impact

We have distributed 50,000+ meals, planted over 20,000 saplings, supported animal welfare initiatives, empowered women in rural areas, and trained over 30,000 interns across multiple domains including IT, finance, marketing, and social work.

Join Us
